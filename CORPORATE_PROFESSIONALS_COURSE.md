## 📋 OVERVIEW

Two-tier AI training program designed for non-technical corporate employees:
- **Track 1A:** Basic AI Literacy (ChatGPT/Copilot fundamentals)
- **Track 1B:** Advanced AI Mastery (Business automation & strategic use)

**Goal:** Empower employees to leverage AI tools for productivity, innovation, and competitive advantage.

### **LEARNING OBJECTIVES**

After completing this course, participants will be able to:

✅ Understand AI fundamentals and real-world applications

### **CURRICULUM BREAKDOWN**

#### **DAY 1: AI FUNDAMENTALS & CHATGPT ESSENTIALS (6 hours)**

**Session 1: AI in the Workplace** (2 hours)
- What is AI? (Demystify the concept)
- AI vs. Automation vs. Machine Learning
- Real-world AI applications in business
- ChatGPT & Copilot overview
- **Activity:** Discuss AI use in their own roles

**Session 2: ChatGPT Basics & Setup** (2 hours)
- Creating and securing a ChatGPT account
- ChatGPT interface walkthrough
- Free vs. paid ChatGPT Plus
- Safety, privacy, and data considerations
- **Activity:** Hands-on: Set up account and first prompts

**Session 3: Prompt Writing 101** (2 hours)
- What is a good prompt?
- The anatomy of an effective prompt (context, task, format)
- Examples and anti-examples
- Common mistakes to avoid
- **Activity:** Hands-on: Write 5 different types of prompts

---

#### **DAY 2: PRACTICAL APPLICATION - DAILY WORK TASKS (6 hours)**

**Session 1: Content Creation & Communication** (2 hours)
- Writing professional emails
- Creating meeting agendas and minutes
- Brainstorming and ideation
- Summarizing documents and meetings
- **Activity:** Write an email, create an agenda using ChatGPT

**Session 2: Document & Report Writing** (2 hours)
- Writing reports and proposals
- Creating data summaries
- Generating marketing content
- Analysis and recommendations
- **Activity:** Draft a business report with ChatGPT

**Session 3: Data Analysis & Insights** (2 hours)
- Using ChatGPT for data interpretation
- Creating presentations from data
- Asking ChatGPT analytical questions
- Spotting patterns and trends
- **Activity:** Analyze sample business data

---

#### **DAY 3 (OPTIONAL): MICROSOFT COPILOT & ADVANCED PROMPTING (6 hours)**

**Session 1: Copilot in Microsoft Office** (2 hours)
- Copilot in Word - document creation & editing
- Copilot in Excel - data analysis & formulas
- Copilot in PowerPoint - presentation creation
- Copilot in Teams & Outlook
- **Activity:** Create a document, spreadsheet, and presentation using Copilot

**Session 2: Advanced Prompting Techniques** (2 hours)
- Chain-of-thought prompting
- Role-based prompting ("Act as an HR manager...")
- Iterative refinement techniques
- Handling edge cases
- **Activity:** Refine prompts for better results

**Session 3: AI Ecosystem & Best Practices** (2 hours)
- Beyond ChatGPT (Copilot, Claude, Perplexity, etc.)
- Choosing the right AI tool for each task
- AI ethics and responsible use
- Limitations and risks of AI
- Future of workplace AI
- **Activity:** Create personalized AI toolkit for their role
