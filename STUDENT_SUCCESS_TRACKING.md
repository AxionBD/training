## EARLY WARNING SYSTEM

**Students flagged for intervention when:**

| Indicator | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Average score <70% | Email: "Let's chat about support options" + Schedule 1:1 |
| 2+ absences | Call/message: "Is everything okay? We miss you" |
| Quiz failure (< 50%) | Recommend: "Consider review sessions with TA" |
| Zero assignment submission | Urgent: Call same day + offer extension |
| Negative feedback | 1:1 with instructor to understand struggle |
| Isolation (quiet in class) | Buddy assignment + peer mentoring |

```
Hi [Student Name],
```

# 🎯 SUCCESS COACHING FRAMEWORK

## FOR AT-RISK STUDENTS (During Program)

**Trigger:** Average score drops below 70% or absence reaches 2+

**Intervention:** 1:1 Success Coaching Session

**Coach Agenda:**
1. **Empathy** (5 min)
   - "How are you feeling about the program?"
   - Listen, don't judge

2. **Assess** (5 min)
   - "What's the main challenge?"
   - Time management? Content confusion? Personal issues?

3. **Action Plan** (10 min)
   - Customize support:
     - Extra tutoring sessions (TA)?
     - Study group assignment?
     - Content review sessions?
     - Personal counseling (if needed)?

4. **Follow-up** (3 min)
   - "Let's check in next Friday"
   - Exchange contact info
   - Set specific goal

**Success Coach Talking Points:**

```
"I noticed your score dropped to 65% last week. 
I want to help you get back on track.

You're definitely capable—I've seen your 
participation and your final projects are solid.

Let's find out what's getting in your way and 
fix it together. No judgment, just support.

What would be most helpful for you right now?"
```
